How To Play

How To Install Maps

  1. Open the game.
  2. Go to the Maps or Installer menu.
  3. Select the map you want to install.
  4. Tap the Install button.
  5. Wait until the download and installation process is complete.
  6. After installation is finished, tap Play.

How To Play

  1. Choose your installed map.
  2. Tap the Play button.
  3. Wait for the world to load.
  4. Start exploring, building, and surviving!

Basic Controls

  • Use joystick to move your character.
  • Tap jump button to jump.
  • Tap attack button to fight enemies.
  • Open inventory to select items and blocks.

Tips

  • Make sure your internet connection is stable when installing maps.
  • Wait until installation is fully completed before pressing Play.
  • Restart the game if the map does not appear after installation.

Enjoy the game and thank you for playing!
